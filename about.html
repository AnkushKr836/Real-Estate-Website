Our Story

Founded in 2015, LuxeHomes was born from a vision to redefine luxury real estate services. What started as a boutique agency has grown into one of the most trusted names in premium property markets across the nation.

Our founder, Alexandra Sterling, recognized a gap in the market for personalized, high-touch service in luxury real estate. With over 15 years of experience and a passion for architecture and design, she assembled a team of industry experts who share her commitment to excellence.

Today, LuxeHomes represents the finest properties in exclusive neighborhoods, serving discerning clients who expect nothing but the best. Our success is built on integrity, expertise, and an unwavering dedication to our clients' dreams.
